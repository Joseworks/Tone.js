Class: MultiSampler

Tone. MultiSampler

Aggregates multiple Tone.Samplers into a single instrument. Pass in a mapping of names to sample urls and an optional callback to invoke when all of the samples are loaded. 

 var sampler = new Tone.MultiSampler({
     "kick" : "../audio/BD.mp3",
     "snare" : "../audio/SD.mp3",
     "hat" : "../audio/hh.mp3"
 }, onload);
 //once loaded...
 sampler.triggerAttack("kick");
